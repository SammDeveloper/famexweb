What is Famex?

FameX is a collectible card bot powered by Discord. It turns these cards into unique collectibles that you can earn, customize, upgrade, trade, and more!

How to play FameX?

Although FameX has dozens of commands, which you can find further on help command, the bot’s two core mechanics are dropping and clamming.
